About Arcana

The best deals do not need more noise.

They need the right people. Arcana sits at the intersection of capital, context and access.

Thesis

Many sophisticated investors have capital but not the time, proximity or market connectivity to source and assess every private opportunity themselves.

Arcana bridges that gap by combining market intelligence, operator insight and trusted relationships into investable conviction.

The best opportunities rarely arrive through public channels. They move through trusted people, informed judgment and timing.

Alexandre Chau
Market Intelligence

Alexandre is a French finance professional with over a decade of sell-side and buy-side experience across Europe and Asia. He holds a Master in Finance from Université Paris Dauphine–PSL.

He began his career at HSBC before moving to Goldman Sachs in London and Hong Kong, and later to Wellington Management, where he managed $12 billion in equity mandates. After becoming crypto native in 2020, he led Wellington’s crypto research and co-authored its institutional paper on digital assets.

At Mythos Liquid Capital he now focuses on systematic strategies and capital preservation.

Thesis

The best deals do not need more noise. They need the right people.

We sit at the intersection of capital, context and access.

Many sophisticated investors have capital but not the time, proximity or market connectivity to source and assess every private opportunity themselves. Arcana bridges that gap by combining market intelligence, operator insight and trusted relationships into investable conviction.

Method

A discreet process for finding signal before consensus.

We are not a volume platform. We focus on opportunities where research, access and relationship capital can compound together.

Form the market map

We build thematic views around sectors, founders and ecosystems where technical, cultural or capital shifts create asymmetric potential.

Validate through people

We test opportunities through trusted operators, investors and counterparties — the people closest to the market and the founder.

Structure the introduction

We connect aligned capital with selected opportunities where the investor can bring more than money: credibility, network, speed or strategic value.

Andrew Law

Partner · Capital & Strategy

Andrew graduated from University College London, ranking among the top of his cohort. Recognised for sharp strategic thinking and hands-on leadership, he combines institutional-level judgment with practical execution across investing, operations and growth.

He co-founded and scaled a direct-to-consumer company into a six-figure enterprise, leading both strategy and operations through its expansion. He later joined Accrete, a private equity firm focused on high-growth technology, where he deepened his experience evaluating and supporting innovative businesses.

Most recently, Andrew served as CIO of LVL Limited, overseeing investments across real estate, technology and traditional assets while advising on broader strategic allocation and capital deployment.

Alexandre Chau

Partner · Market Intelligence

Alexandre is a French finance professional with more than a decade of experience across both the sell-side and buy-side in Europe and Asia. He holds a Master in Finance from Université Paris Dauphine–PSL and has built a career spanning global banking, institutional asset management and digital assets.

He began his career at HSBC before joining Goldman Sachs across London and Hong Kong, later moving to Wellington Management where he managed over $12 billion in equity mandates. Following his transition into digital assets in 2020, he led Wellington’s crypto research initiatives and co-authored the firm’s institutional paper on digital assets.

Alexandre currently focuses on systematic strategies, market structure and capital preservation through his work at Mythos Liquid Capital.
